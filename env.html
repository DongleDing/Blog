  • ..The crazy ones..
    The misfits
    The rebels
    The troublemakers
    The round pegs in the squareholes
    The ones who see things differently
    They're not fond of rules, and they have no respect to the status quo You can quote them, disagree with them, glorify or vilify them.
    About the only thing you can't do is to ignore them, because they change things, they push the human race forward.
    While some may see them as the crazy ones, we see genius.
    Because the people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world,
    are the ones who do.
