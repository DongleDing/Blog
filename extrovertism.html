Extroversion: Something that shouldn't be force fed

As an individual who prefers mild space and a small friend circle, it is indeed a necessity to find a space for an introvert like me to help recharge social battery and go wild once it's filled. And it wasn't that necessary for Aadhila to express her shock when I ushered myself into that wilderness when Shalmali Kholgade took on the stage during Hestia Tech Fest, and when everyone including her went nuts jumping around like if its a concert where Taylor Swift heards her flock of sheep. (rip my limb, swifties, I'll wait). All I need is a ukulele, and a Honda Africa Twin. Why? That's the best bike for long haul. Why a ukulele? I don't think any middle class guitarists take their Les Paul or Fenders into the road trip.
